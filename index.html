Lakhi Narayan Bharadwaj

Ph.D. Candidate in Economics · IIT Guwahati

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at IIT Guwahati under the supervision of Prof. Amarjyoti Mahanta. My research examines the labor market implications of technological change, focusing on its impact on employment composition and wage share in India's organized and unorganized manufacturing sectors.

My broader research interests include labor economics and development economics, particularly technological change, labor demand, and workforce composition in developing economies.

I hold an M.Phil. and M.A. in Economics from Dibrugarh University, Assam (CGPA: 8.11).
