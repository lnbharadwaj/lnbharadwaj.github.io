About Me

I am a PhD researcher working on advanced machine learning methods for structured and multimodal data. My research integrates deep learning, network science, and applied statistics.

Research Agenda

Technological Change and Organized Manufacturing

Examining the impact of technological change on employment and wage share in the Indian organized manufacturing sector. The analysis distinguishes between production and managerial workers, with further disaggregation of production workers into permanent and contractual categories.

Technological Change and Unorganized Manufacturing

Investigating how technological change affects employment structure and wage share in the Indian unorganized manufacturing sector, with a focus on hired workers and unpaid family workers.

Inter-Sectoral Spillovers

Analyzing whether technological change in the organized manufacturing sector influences employment dynamics and expansion in the unorganized sector, highlighting structural linkages between the two segments.
